# CyberGuard AI — build and submission plan

## One-sentence pitch
CyberGuard AI is a safe multi-agent triage assistant that helps small organisations
convert a cyber concern into a transparent risk priority and human-reviewed defensive
action plan.

## Target user
A small organisation without a dedicated security team: a community group, small
business, school club, charity, or local service provider.

## Demonstration scenario
“A staff member clicked a suspicious link in an email on a shared laptop but did not
enter a password. They are worried it was a payroll scam.”

## What judges should see
- A clear social-impact problem.
- Four specialised ADK agents in a `SequentialAgent`.
- A local read-only FastMCP server.
- A working Streamlit interface.
- Privacy/security decisions reflected in the code.
- An honest limitation statement and human-in-the-loop escalation.
